VAPT 

A vulnerability assessment is the process of determining, identifying and prioritizing vulnerabilities in computer systems, applications, and network infrastructures and moreover providing the organization undergoing the assessment with the fundamental knowledge, awareness, and risk background to recognize the threats to its environment and respond suitably.

Incident Response 

Incident response is an organized strategy for approaching and managing the aftermath of a security breach or cyberattack, also known as an IT Incident, Computer Incident or Security Incident. The intention is to supervise the circumstances in a style that restrains damage and reduces recovery time and expenses.

Secure Infrastructure 

The security measures to shield infrastructures, especially critical infrastructures, such as network communications, communication center, server center, database center, and IT center.Infrastructure security endeavors to limit the vulnerability of these structures and systems from sabotage and corruption.
